Myers Briggs Type Indicator - MBTI

Psychometric Test – to know the personality type/style.
MBTI is not an indicator of capability and that’s why it is not used for recruitment.
E & I and J & P indicates how one orient with the external world.
S & N and T & F is about how you think internally – Mental Functions.

Extrovert, Introvert
Sensing, Intuition
Thinking, Feeling
Judging, Perceiving

Nature Vs Nurture – Few characteristics are nature and few has to be nurtured.


Quality Relationships Model (QRM)

The leader plays 4 roles in QRM based on the situation and need.
Teaching, Coaching, Mentoring and Counselling.

  • Teaching (SME)
  • Coaching (Facilitation)
  • Mentoring (Sharing Experience)
  • Counselling (address specific problem/issue)

GROW Model

A structured approach to coaching for developmental and performance improvement.
Goal – what you want to achieve
Reality – assess the situation
Options – identify options to make progress
Will – accountability for actions to accomplish the goal

SMART goal – Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound.


Core Communications Skills for Leaders

  1. Face-to-face communication
    • Body language and Vocal/Tone takes 93% of any communication. Verbal is just 7%.
  2. Listening
    • Listening; not just hearing!
    • Listening Techniques – Bracketing, Questioning, Paraphrasing, Tracking and Trusting Silence.
  3. Questioning Techniques
    • Open-ended Qs – What, When, Why and How.
    • Closed-ended Qs – Who, Yes/No, True/False, etc.,
    • Clarifying Qs
    • Multi-leveled Qs

Feedback

Feedback should be continuous and two way.
Feedback should encourage the person to move forward.

  1. Six steps for giving Quality Feedback
    • Find the right time & place
    • Describe Situation
    • Outline the observed behaviour
    • Explain the impact
    • Pause for Response & Explore Together
    • Discover new possibilities!
  2. Receive Feedback
    • Know yourself
    • Understand yourself
    • Manager your reactions
    • Separate the Content from the giver
    • Listen, Summarize, ask
    • Thank the giver
    • Try out a small change
